How To

Are you ready to make a decision? Here's what to do:

  1. Select a category from the home page or under "Make a Decision" in the navigation bar. We've got you covered if you're deciding which college to attend, which house you should buy, or which restaurant to go to. You can also use a blank template (called "Other") to decide on anything.
  2. You will be brought to a page with a chart to help you make your decision. On the blank chart, type in your question where it says "Big Question".
  3. In the italicized blocks in the top row of the chart, type in the options you are trying to decide between. To add or remove more options, click the teal "Add Option" or pink "Remove Option" button.
  4. Look at the leftmost column. On the pre-made charts, Factors (things that you consider when you make a decision) are already there. On the "Other" chart, type in factors in each box. You can also add additional Factors by clicking the purple "Add Factor" button on any chart.
  5. Rate how important each factor is to you in the "Weight of Factor" column on a scale from 1 to 10. Higher numbers indicate greater importance.
  6. For each factor, rate your options on a scale of 1 to 10, with 10 being the best. If you do not select a number, then the score will be considered 0.
  7. Finally, click the "Decide" button below the chart. The scores for each option will be displayed under the button. Higher scores indicate a better choice for you.
